Can You Tell What's Made Up?


An interactive quiz designed to test how well context can be interpreted from pictures

Abstract

After reviewing Photographs of Agony for my second progression, I decided to make this quiz in order to try to understand how context can be manipulated around a photograph. In addition, this quiz can aid in understanding how useful a photograph is in providing clear information when standing on its own.

How It Works

In this quiz, you will be shown multiple images.
These images will be paired with three different captions
One of these captions was used to accompany the image in a news article, while the other two are fake
You will be able to pick which caption you think was the correct one
In the end, you will be able to see your results and score.
